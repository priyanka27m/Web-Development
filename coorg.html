Coorg Tour

Nestled in the beautiful hills and profound valleys of Karnataka, Coorg is a renowned tourist destination. The town is blessed with picturesque charm and scenic vistas. Other than the alluring atmosphere, this mesmerizing hill station is affluent with cultural and historical backdrop. A tour to Coorg makes the tourists dip into a unique world of serenity and nature's bounty.

This 03 Nights and 04 Days itinerary leads to a tour that covers visiting Coorg in a comprehensive way. The major highlights of this trip are visiting Coorg city, exploring ancient Omkareshwar Temple, witnessing exotic natural beauty at Raja's Seat and Abbey's Falls, and savouring Tibetan flavours at Golden Temple. This tour is a perfect option for travellers to visit some of the most consecrated temples and enjoy a joyous tour to nature's fascinating Eden..
