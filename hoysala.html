Hoysala Tour

Hoysala architecture is the structural design that was developed under the reign of Hoysala Empire between 11th and 14th century. In this reign, some beautiful temples were constructed in various regions in South India such as Halebid, Belur, and Somanathapura. These temples thus boast magnificent architecture and ancient significance as well. The temples made by Hoysala Empire have a 'manatapa', which is a hall where swarms of devotees gather during prayers. These manatapas are one of the salient features of these temples.

This 02 Nights and 03 Days itinerary is a comprehensive tour package to Hoysala temples. The major destinations covered in this tour are Somanathapura, Mysore, Halebid, Belur, and Kaidala. The major highlight of the trip is visiting the beautiful architecture, serenity, and spirituality in the Hoysala temples such as Chennakesava Temple, Kaidala Chennakesava Temple, and more.
